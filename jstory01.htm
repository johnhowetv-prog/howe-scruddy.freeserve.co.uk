"Suddenly I saw them coming through the window"

by John Michael Howe (11/12/2000)

Once there was a gigantic window in New York. It was the year 2100.

Aliens came through the window and attacked earth. There was only ten Aliens. The leader was a spider with yellow and black legs. It had a red horn on its head. Its width is 15m and its height is 50m.

The others were just the same except they were smaller.

They were entering New York city. The leader went up the Empire State building, pushed Godzilla off and roared. Unfortunately when Godzilla hit the ground it wiped out three Aliens so there was only seven Aliens left.

Then humans started going in helicopters and shooting them but it was no use the Aliens just kept spitting electric web at them and they crashed into tall buildings, but one crashed into the ground and it wiped out two Aliens.

There was green and black army tanks shooting the leader Alien, it was no use the Aliens kept destroying New York city.

The little Aliens sucked humans out of the Army tanks. The Army tanks kept going forward and ran over four Aliens so there were only the leader Aliens left.

So they got a helicopter and carried it to the middle of the Atlantic Ocean dropped him put an atom bomb on him, the bomb exploded but it was still no use so the leader Alien swam back to New York and started Attacking.

Off a building a pole snapped off and ripped the web apart and the leader Alien fell at 10,000,000,000 miles an hour, so New York was safe.

Until Aliens attack Australia!

The End
