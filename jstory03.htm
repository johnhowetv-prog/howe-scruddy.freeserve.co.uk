"Saving Animals From Extinction"

by John Michael Howe (21/08/2001)

One hundred miles away, a giant comet with poisonous animal acid surrounding it was being sucked up by a super massive black hole.

The impact of this changed its course to head straight to earth !

On the very same night, a boy called Ben was celebrating his tenth birthday. His Nan gave him a large present. He ripped the paper off - it was a junior telescope. He took all the parts out of the box and started to build it.

When he finished he took it outside to see the stars. He saw a bright white star that was moving closer every minute. He told his mum, so she had a look, and when she saw it move about five times, she swiftly ran inside and picked up the phone. She phoned "Pirate FM" (their local radio station), and told them "a giant comet is going to hit earth".

Astronaut people were listening to it as well, but when they heard about the comet, they thought straight away "radar gun". The gun was massive and more powerful than an atom bomb. They spotted the co-ordinates and shot. It missed and hit Jupiter. They shot again. It missed again and hit Jupiter. Fifteen minutes later it has shot about 50 bullets, and they have all hit Jupiter. They had one more bang ... there it goes ... hit Jupiter ... but then Jupiter explodes, and wipes out the comet, all because of a gun - but the boy had saved all of the animals in the world !

The End
